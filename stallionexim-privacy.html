Privacy Policy

The Scribble Club  |  Last updated: May 2026

The Scribble Club ("we", "our", or "us") operates book publishing and promotion activities under Stallion Exim, based in Delhi, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our social media integrations and publishing tools.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to operate our publishing and social media scheduling tools. This may include:

— Social media account identifiers (Pinterest, Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter) used solely to post content on our own accounts.
— Basic analytics data (post performance, impressions) provided by platform APIs.
— No personal data of third-party users is collected or stored.

2. How We Use Information

All data collected is used exclusively for:

— Scheduling and publishing book promotion content to our own social media accounts.
— Tracking post performance for internal reporting only.
— We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

3. Third-Party Platforms

Our tools interact with the following platforms via their official APIs: Pinterest, Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter). Use of these platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies. We access only the permissions necessary for content publishing on our own accounts.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on our private systems in Delhi, India. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond the official API calls required for publishing.

5. Data Retention

Post logs and session data are retained for internal operational purposes only and are not shared externally. Data is deleted when no longer operationally required.

6. Your Rights

As we do not collect personal data from third parties, there is no user data to request, modify, or delete. If you have questions about data related to our social media activity, please contact us.
